# SmartFence

<h1>SmartFence API and Webpage</h1>
<h3>Makes management of your less "smart" mower easier and handy</h3>


Some "smart" mowers uses electrified wires in the ground as boundaries of your garden.
 With this you can have your old smart mower have different regions of your garden.
 
 
<h1>USAGE</h1>
  - Split your garden into different regions and toggle them from the bed or automatic by time!
